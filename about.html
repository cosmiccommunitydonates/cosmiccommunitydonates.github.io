The Cosmic Community

We spend a lot of our time looking up at the sky as scientists and lovers of physics and astronomy. As citizens, we think now is an important time to turn our attention to Earth and invest in our communities in an impactful way.

Join us!

We hope this donation to the American Civil Liberties Union enables all our neighbors to live free of fear, preserves their right to peacefully protest, and helps make their voices heard.

Our donors are among the scientists and science lovers who agree the ACLU has the potential to help realize these hopes to support our fellow citizens. For future donation drives, the Cosmic Community organizers are looking at other charities that benefit all people regardless of wealth, color, creed, genetics, or who they love.

The views expressed by the Cosmic Community organizers are not representative of any federally funded science organization, foundation, or collaboration.
