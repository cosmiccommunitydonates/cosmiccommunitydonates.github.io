The Cosmic Community

As scientists and lovers of physics and astronomy, we spend a lot of our time looking up at the sky. We recognize that science is done by people, and it is also important to turn our attention to Earth and support our communities in an impactful way.

Join us in our united gift to the ACLU

Our donors include scientists and science lovers who want our colleagues and neighbors to live free of fear of persecution and who want to preserve our common right to express our opinions peacefully without fear of retaliation.

Our donations to the American Civil Liberties Union will help realize these hopes to support our colleagues and fellow citizens, regardless of wealth, color, creed, genetics, or who they love.

The views expressed by the Cosmic Community organizers are not representative of any federally funded science organization, foundation, or collaboration.
